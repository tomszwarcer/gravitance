An interactive n-body simulator intended to give intuition for the motion of massive bodies under gravity.

To run: `python3 gravitance.py`

There are two modes to the game. You can toggle between them by pressing j.

Add mode: add bodies by clicking and holding. The mass increases the longer you hold. While clicked, drag the mouse cursor to shoot the body in the direction of the line.

Bind mode: click on a body and drag to another body to bind the two bodies in an orbit around their centre of mass.
